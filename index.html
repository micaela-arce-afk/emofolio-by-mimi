MIKAN ミカン

I’m Mikan, a visual designer and illustrator with a strong interest in audiovisual creation and graphic experimentation. I studied Audiovisual Design and Illustration at UDIT, where I developed a multidisciplinary approach combining image, narrative, and technology. My work focuses on digital illustration, motion graphics, editorial design, and interactive visuals. I mainly work with the Adobe Creative Suite, along with tools such as Cinema 4D, Figma, Visual Studio Code, and Clip Studio Paint. I’m always exploring new visual languages and creative challenges to keep growing as a designer.
